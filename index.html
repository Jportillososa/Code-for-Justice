Affordable Housing For All...

Home. The place where we lay our heads, hug our loved ones, make our memories regardless of what we look like or how much is in our wallets.
We must come together across racial differences to rewrite laws to protect us, keep a roof over our heads, and create an environment where our rent is reasonable and housing options are plentiful. We make the way for a better future.

    About STAY STAY is a non-profit organization that focuses on restablishing afforable housing for those who can't afford it. STAY has a mission of helping those in need during these hard times. At STAY, we believe that housing is a human right, not a commodity to be bought and sold for profit.
